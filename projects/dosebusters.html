DoseBusters (VR)

Unity • C# • Blender

What it is

DoseBusters is a VR learning experience that teaches radiation principles through interactive gameplay. My work focused on the shielding mini-game and the systems that made levels scalable and maintainable.

My contributions

  • Designed and implemented core mechanics: shielding, dosimetry, and NPC interactions.
  • Built an in-game tablet for navigation and objective tracking with maintainable scripts and documentation.
  • Helped create procedurally generated shielding levels to improve replayability and engagement.
